About RMS Portal

Empowering Academic Integrity at Metropolitan University

The Research Management System (RMS) is a specialized platform developed to streamline the academic research submission process. Our mission is to provide students and faculty of Metropolitan University with a robust tool for real-time plagiarism analysis and efficient reviewer feedback.

Automated analysis to ensure your research work is original and meets academic standards.

Track your submission status from 'Pending' to 'Approved' with an interactive visual dashboard.

📝 Easy Citation

Generate APA style citations instantly for your approved research papers with one click.
