**A specialized workflow management system for academic research submissions and feedback.**

---

## 🛠 Features
- **Student Module:** Secure submission of research papers and real-time status tracking.
- **Teacher/Reviewer Module:** Specialized dashboard to review submissions, assign status (Pending/Approved/Rejected), and provide qualitative feedback.
- **Automated Workflow:** Dynamic badge system based on paper status.
- **Search & Filter:** Easily find specific research papers from the database.
